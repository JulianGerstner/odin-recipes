Pastel de papa (Potato pie)

Description

Pastel de papas is like a spicy version of shepherd's pie with the addition of Chilean cheese. It's hearty and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter, or to taste
  • salto to taste
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 pounde ground beef
  • 2 tablespoon shredded Panquehue cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 pinch cayenne peper
  • salt and black pepper to taste
